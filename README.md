# 💜 Calmivo — AI Mental Wellness Companion

> Calmivo is an AI-powered mental wellness companion designed to offer emotional support, mood tracking, and evidence-based coping tools — accessible anytime, for free.

🌐 **Live App:** https://calmivo.vercel.app/

---

## 🌿 Why I Built Calmivo

Mental health support should be accessible, stigma-free, and available 24/7.

Calmivo is my attempt to bridge the gap between:
- 🕐 Immediate emotional support  
- 📊 Self-awareness through mood tracking  
- 📚 Evidence-based wellness techniques  

It’s not a replacement for therapy.  
It’s a supportive digital companion when someone needs to talk.

---

## 🧠 What Calmivo Can Do

### 💬 AI Companion — “Sage”

Sage is a compassionate AI built on Claude that:
- Listens without judgment  
- Encourages reflection  
- Uses CBT-style reframing  
- Suggests grounding & mindfulness techniques  
- Reminds users it’s an AI, not a licensed therapist  

The goal is emotional safety, not medical advice.

---

### 🚨 Crisis Detection

Every message is scanned for high-risk distress signals.

If detected:
- Emergency resources (988, Crisis Text Line) are surfaced immediately  
- The tone shifts to supportive and grounding  
- The user is encouraged to seek real-world help  

Safety is always prioritized.

---

### 📊 Mood Tracker

Users can:
- Log daily emotional states  
- Add notes and tags  
- View mood trends over time  
- See summaries and patterns  

The idea: awareness → patterns → better coping decisions.

---

### 📚 Wellness Resource Library

Curated, categorized tools including:
- Breathing exercises  
- Grounding techniques  
- Sleep support  
- Mindfulness practices  

Designed to be practical, simple, and immediately usable.

---

## 🎯 Product Philosophy

Calmivo is built around four principles:

1. Safety First  
2. Non-judgmental AI  
3. Privacy-conscious design  
4. Simple, frictionless experience  

No sign-up required.  
No paywall.  
No overcomplication.

## ⚠️ Important Disclaimer

Calmivo is **not** a substitute for professional mental health care.

If you are in immediate crisis in the U.S.:
- Call or text **988** (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline)  
- Text **HOME** to **741741** (Crisis Text Line)  

If outside the U.S., please contact local emergency services.
